When I turned eighteen I decided to move back to Sweden in order to continue my education. I had a slight problem however, I didn't really know what I wanted to do.

I'm a guy with many interests, and a very particular taste in movies, so I always have a lot going on. My interests include running, gaming, sketching and talking to complete strangers. If you're ever sitting next to me on the buss, don't hesitate to say Hi!
