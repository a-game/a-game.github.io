I'm a curious and observant fellow, who likes to solve problems and figure out how things really work.

I'm a year old dude living Malmö, Sweden, where I was born and spent the first nine years of my life. At the age of nine I moved to Cairo with my dad, where I spent another nine years in a metropolis that never sleeps.

Growing up in such a huge city taught me a lot about diplomacy and handling people, because you are surrounded by them all the time. Another important feature I've gained from my years in Cairo is coping with stress and a life of fast pace.

When I turned eighteen I decided to move back to Sweden in order to continue my education. I had a slight problem however, I didn't really know what I wanted to do.

I have two passions in life, solving problems and understanding how things tick. At a young age I used to take my dad's old gadgets, take them apart and try to draw up diagrams of how then were constructed and why it behaves in a certain way when you press one button or another. This led me to the realization that I wanted to do something technical, probably something with computers which I found particularly fascinating. I've always been good with people though so I really wanted to have some social aspect to my line of work.
